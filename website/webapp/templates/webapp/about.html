What is Medseer?

 Medseer is an evolving AI powered search engine that helps users find trusted resources for health-related questions. Medseer uses advances in natural language processing and machine learning  to extract and highlights answers to users' questions using scientific literature at the backend.  

Contact: This project is developed and hosted by University of Basel. For any inquires please contact dina.awad@unibas.ch

What is Medseer objective?

Medseer aims to improve the quality of search results and help users avoid misinformation in the health domain, which can be very risky to gather such information from unprofessional and untrusted  sources. Medseer focuses primarily on the literature in the health domain which indicates a certain level of quality. Scientific literature from reputable sources like conferences and journal contains rich  information that has undergone proper review to guarantee its content quality. Scientific literature is authored by subject matter experts in their fields which is a good indicator for the knowledge  experience in a particular domain.  Medseer uses these sources at its backend to harvest knowledge with reliable credibility. Currently, Medseer covers literature in the dermatology domain and intends  to expand to other health domains.
